Rice Recipe

Now we are going to make a rice recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Rice
  • 200 grm onion
  • 200 grm tomato
  • 2 teaspoon slt
  • 250 grm oil
  • 50 grm gram msala

Method

  1. first of all keep the rice in the water for some time
  2. cut the onion & tomato in small pieces or grind it
  3. put the pressure cukar on the burner and put the oil, past of tomato, onion in it
  4. put the salt & garam masala in the past and mix it with the spoon
  5. mix the rice with the past properly
  6. keep it on the burner for 10 or 15 mints
  7. hellow my rice recepe is ready for represent
